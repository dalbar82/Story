ABOUT ME

×

Christmas 1994. A rich uncle gave me (then 12 years old) and all my cousins a Walkman. It was a big deal - we didn’t have much money back then. Inside a month I had pulled that Walkman apart, screw by screw. I needed to know how it worked!

Since that day, I have never stopped tinkering, and whilst I’ve moved from machinery to technology, my love for learning how things work and creating new things still pushes my buttons.  

At 18, I combined my love of playing and writing music with my love of tech by completing a Bachelor of Music Technology. I went out into the world, with hopes of rock stardom, but also with the skills to work in the Music industry in a technical capacity.  

It has taken many years, a career change and becoming a father, to realise what I really want to do is code. Combining my technical knowledge, in-depth problem-solving abilities, and need to create, it is the perfect fit.

Now I am all about the code. I still write music as a hobby, but spend most of my time coding different projects and learning new coding languages.
